Article Attachment

Resource for Attachments of an Article, typically images for an Article.

Operations

  • POST rest/v1/trade-memberships/{membershipId}/articles/{articleId}/attachments/{attachmentId}
  • GET rest/v1/trade-memberships/{membershipId}/articles/{articleId}/attachments/
  • DELETE rest/v1/trade-memberships/{membershipId}/articles/{articleId}/attachments/

Authorization

  • All operations can be performed by clients with a valid Authorization header
