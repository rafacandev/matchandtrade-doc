Article Attachment

Resource for Attachments of an Article

This resource only manages the one-to-many relationship between Articles and Attachments. Therefore the POST and DELETE operations only create or delete the relationship.

Refer to Attachments resource for more operations.

Operations

  • POST rest/v1/articles/{articleId}/attachments/{attachmentId}
  • GET rest/v1/articles/{articleId}/attachments/
  • DELETE rest/v1/articles/{articleId}/attachments/{attachmentId}

Authorization

  • All operations can be performed by clients with a valid Authorization header
