Use Cases

This page describes a typical use case where a User creates a Trade Users submit and match Articles and finally generate the TradeResults

Olavo is a User which Authenticates on the system. Note that the returned Authorization header needs to be included in all secured endpoints.

${OWNER_AUTHENTICATE}

Olavo gets his Authentication details. Note that Olavo will need know his User.userId in the future.

${OWNER_AUTHENTICATIONS}

Olavo creates a Trade, by default he becomes the organizer of the Trade. The Trade.state is SUBMITTING_ARTICLES meaning that other Users are allowed to become members and submit Articles.

${OWNER_TRADES_POST}

Olavo gets his TradeMemberhip details.

${OWNER_MEMBERSHIP}

Olavo adds two Articles to his TradeMemberhip.

${OWNER_ARTICLE_ONE} ${OWNER_ARTICLE_TWO}

Maria Authenticates on the system. Note that the returned Authorization header needs to be included in all secured endpoints.

${MEMBER_AUTHENTICATE}

Maria gets her Authentication details. Note that Maria will need know her User.userId in the future.

${MEMBER_AUTHENTICATIONS}

Maria subscribes to Olavo's Trade via TradeMemberhip endpoint.

${MEMBER_TRADES_MEMBERSHIP}

Maria adds three Articles to her TradeMemberhip.

${MEMBER_ARTICLE_ONE} ${MEMBER_ARTICLE_TWO} ${MEMBER_ARTICLE_THREE}

Olavo changes the Trade.state to MATCHING_ARTICLES. Note that only Trade owners can change the Trade.state

${TRADE_MATCHING_ARTICLES}

Olavo matches his Articles with Articles from other members via the WantArticles endpoint.
He offers Pandemic Legacy: Season 1 and wants Maria's Stone Age in exchange.

${OWNER_OFFER_ONE}

Maria matches her Articles with Articles from other members via the WantArticles endpoint.
She offers Stone Age and wants Olavo's Pandemic Legacy: Season 1 in exchange.
She also offers Stone Age and wants Olavo's Pandemic Legacy: Season 2 in exchange.

${MEMBER_OFFER_ONE} ${MEMBER_OFFER_TWO}

Olavo changes the Trade.state to GENERATE_TRADES.

${TRADE_GENERATE_TRADES}

Olavo gets the TradeResults which says:
Olavo (offering_user_id=1) sends Pandemic Legacy: Season 1 (offering_article_id=1) to Maria (receiving_user_id=2) in exchange for Stone Age (receiving_article_id=3)
Maria (offering_user_id=2) sends Stone Age (offering_article_id=3) to Olavo (receiving_user_id=1) in exchange for Pandemic Legacy: Season 1 (receiving_article_id=1)
Note that any member of a Trade can get TradeResults.

${TRADE_RESULTS}
